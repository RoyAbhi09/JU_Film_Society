Chiaroscuro

Chiaroscuro, our vibrant film magazine by Jadavpur University Film Society, celebrates the multifaceted artistry of cinema. Just like the dramatic interplay of light and dark in classic films, we hope our magazine embodies the essence of light and shadow in storytelling. The labor of our love, Chiaroscuro, aims to enrich the cultural discourse by cultivating the understanding and appreciation of films among readers.
